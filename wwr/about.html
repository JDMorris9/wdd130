History

Once upon a time, we had a life that was, well,... lame. So lame was the lameness that we nearly died. From that lame-nitudity was born a dream: a dream that one day, all lameness would cease! No one should ever have to have a life that is lame! So we started a business, utilizing the least lame thing we could think of.

... When we couldn't get the licensing to lay waste to the surface dwellers and to upend all semblance of physics and gravity as we know it, we settled on whitewater rafting instead. So we hope you think it's just as cool as we do, and, you know, stuff. Even though it was our second choice, we hope it will be your first!

What's Included / What to Bring

We'll provide all the equipment you will need for this adventure of a lifetime! This includes a life vest, paddle, and helmet. Meals are always provided, unless you're a vegan or a cannibal--in which case, you can expect to fend for yourself. All you will need to bring is:

  • Yourself
  • A swimsuit, or clothes to get wet in (no jeans or cotton)
  • Two forks
  • Your social security card
  • Your entire life savings (or that of your children, if you prefer)
  • The head of medusa
  • And a propensity for pandemonium!

We can't wait to take this adventure with you!

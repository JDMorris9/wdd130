Our Trips

Like having a ridiculous number of options? Well have we got a deal for YOU! Check out this variety of exciting trips to choose from!

Known for its scenic mudbanks and occasional government surveillance canoes, the Mucky Duck Circuit features surprise whirlpools, award-winning splash choreography, a taste of our very own world-famous duck jerky, and the occasional airborne paddle. Get ready for a trip most fowl.

The River Monster River is a heart-pounding thrill ride of potentially deadly and illogical twists and turns! Despite its name, it does not actually feature a river monster. (Any sightings are purely coincidental and legally unverifiable.)

With its glittering waters, semi-cooperative wildlife, and mysterious musical interludes, the Snorky Creek tour is one of our highest rated trips, and is sure to be one of your favorites too! Just be sure to ignore the singing fruits, and never accept maps from talking fish. They will try to mislead you.
